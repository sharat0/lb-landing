    We offer a wide range of services !

    Our platform encompasses a comprehensive range of tools necessary for efficiently managing educational institutions, encompassing crucial aspects such as student enrollment, learning management system, student information, attendance system, tests, faculty management, lab management, chatroom, and more. Let us now delve into each of these components individually to gain a deeper understanding.

    Connect New

    Our "Connect" feature serves as a central hub for all kinds of important information like latest event announcements, important dates, or syllabus updates, ensuring everyone in same or different institutions at our plaatform stays connected.

    Student Management

    Our student management system simplifies the process of student enrollment enabling seamless sharing of learning content, results, and granting access to labs with just a few clicks.

    Faculty Management

    Our faculty management system streamlines the administrative tasks related to managing faculty members within your institution like faculty recruitment, assignment of courses, scheduling, and tracking attendance.

    LMS

    We have a flexible learning management system for smooth and easy to manage course content that brings together learning, growth, skills, and capabilities into a personalized, curated experience.

    Attendance

    Our easy to use online attendance system make taking attendance quick and less prone to errors. Record a student’s attendance with a click of the mouse and the data is visible immediately to students as well as parents.

    Results

    Our platform makes it easy to update results on few clicks without fear of errors. Record a student’s results with a click of the mouse and the data is visible immediately to students as well as parents.
